About Al Moruff Enterprise

Your trusted partner for industrial sewing machines and spare parts.

Our Mission

To provide top-quality sewing machines and accessories, ensuring excellence and reliability for all garment-making needs.

Our Vision

To become the leading supplier of industrial sewing solutions, empowering businesses and individuals with state-of-the-art products.

Our Journey

Al Moruff Enterprise started as a small family-owned business dedicated to supplying sewing enthusiasts with quality tools. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in industrial sewing machines and spare parts, serving both local and international clients.
